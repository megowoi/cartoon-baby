Eren Yeager

About Eren

A young German boy who lost his mother to the Titans and vows to avenge her by killing all the Titans. He has beautiful green eyes and always causes trouble for Mikasa.

Eren's characteristics

  1. He's adorable
  2. He's stronger than most people give him credit for (Top 5 of his graduating class!!)
  3. He cherishes his friends and family
  4. He's too innocent for his own good

Eren was granted the ability to transform into a man-eating Titan.
